Logistics

Industrial district: logistic area, offices

The industrial district is very important for the functionality of a city. Each individual part plays a separate yet important role. The logistics area helps all operational run smoothly behinds the scenes. Not only does it help coordinate and organise the city, but it also creates jobs, increases efficiency and overall improves the city ever so slightly. The efficiency of a logistics area also more importantly helps reduce carbon emissions.

Logistic Marina

The logistics marina help make use of the sea access. By using energy efficient port operations and making use of hybrid or electric vehicles it will help a marina stay sustainable.

Finance Depeartment: banks

Finance departments give all residents of a city access to their funds. To be more energy efficient banks can use renewable energy and energy efficient lighting to reduce their carbon footprint.
