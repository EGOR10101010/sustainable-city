City Plan

We have created a city plan based on a real location of the coast of Jakarta in Indonesia. The reason we have choose this location is simply because they have a multitude of issues concerning poverty and poor waste management and a whole list of issues that helped us choose them. The city plan was made using 3DCityPlanner software which allowed us to make a full 3D model of our city plan. We have added 20 different interactive elements that all showcase a separate building or function all the way from trams to hospitals. We choose an artificial island as one of Jakarta's main issues is terrible air quality. By making an artificial island we allow a lot of space for free air from the sea.
