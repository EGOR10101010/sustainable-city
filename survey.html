Survey Answers

We created a survey in the attempt of gaining some guidance from people in general regarding what they want to learn about the environment and sustainability. We gathered 57 responses, and we were required to gain 45. The responses to the survey told us that public transport was very important. Many people use it as was seen by a 7.44 average response regarding use of public transport. This is just one example of how the survey influenced our actions regarding creating the city plan.
