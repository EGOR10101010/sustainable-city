Housing

    Apartments

  • Apartments are becoming very popular due to their cost-effective nature. They help maximise land usage, but they also reduce the energy used as people share walls. The apartment buildings also have green areas outside to maintain a vibrant and more economically friendly environment for the residents.

    Estates Houses

  • Most estates offer a good ratio of green to the amount of housing. Although they may not be as efficient and effective as apartment buildings it's not unheard of to build the houses themselves out of shipping containers per say. Building an estate of energy efficient homes can see great outcomes for sustainability as well as functionality.
